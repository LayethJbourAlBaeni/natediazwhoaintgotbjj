Who is Nate Diaz?

Nate Diaz—or more formally, Nathan Donald Diaz— is a legendary figure in the world of mixed martial arts (MMA) and more recently, professional boxing. Born on April 16, 1985, in Stockton, California, he’s known for his gritty fighting style, sharp wit, and unapologetic attitude inside and outside the cage.

Career highlights

- UFC Icon: Diaz rose to fame after winning The Ultimate Fighter 5 in 2007, which launched his long and colorful career in the UFC.
- Submission Specialist: He’s especially known for his Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu skills, with many of his wins coming via submission—his signature move being the triangle choke.
- Bonus Machine: He holds the third-highest number of UFC post-fight bonus awards (16 total), including Fight of the Night and Performance of the Night.
- Boxing Debut: In 2023, Diaz stepped into the boxing ring against Jake Paul. Though he lost by unanimous decision, it marked a new chapter in his combat sports journey.
